How to make a Sandwich?

- Ingredients:

       1. Bread slices (white, brown, or whole wheat)
       2. Butter
       3. Green chutney (optional, but adds great flavor)
       4. Mayonnaise (optional)
       5. Tomato ketchup (optional)

- Steps to prepare Sandwich:

        1. Take two slices of bread.
        2. Spread butter on both slices.
        3. Apply green chutney or mayonnaise (optional).
        4. Place sliced cucumber, tomato, onion, and boiled potato on one bread slice.
        5. Sprinkle salt, black pepper, and chaat masala over the vegetables.
        6. Add a cheese slice or grated cheese (optional).
        7. Cover with the second bread slice.
        8. Cut the sandwich into two halves.
        9. Serve fresh, or grill/toast it for 2–3 minutes if desired.
